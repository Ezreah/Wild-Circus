Wildcat's show

Tabayara Maluenda, aka “Taba,” is a sixth-generation circus performer who knows how to build trust with tigers. Performing for Wild Cirucs is Taba’s dream come true. Every day he looks forward to the moment when the arena darkens and the spotlight beams upon his majestic big cats. Taba’s infectious energy, charisma and passion for performing knows no bounds, as Taba sees family where others see danger! Taba and his entourage of 16 exotic Bengal tigers demonstrate an intimate bond between human and animal that is unforgettable!

Acrobats

Acrobatics is a great skill to offer at any event because every member of your audience can relate to it. Whilst other circus skills generally involve a prop – acrobatics often just uses the human body – and the impressive stunts performed by the artists really resonate with the crowd. We offer both tumbling (flips and spins) as well as duo or group acro-balance acts. Our acrobats are available for both stage shows as well as walkabout performance.
